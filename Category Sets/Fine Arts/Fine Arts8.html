1. Fine Arts/All (HSAPQ NSC 2 2008)
This piece features superpositions of an E flat dominant seventh chord. It includes rarely-heard instruments like the guiro, which can be heard in the section "Procession of the Sage," and ends with a young girl dancing herself to death. This piece begins with a melody adapted from a Lithunanian folk song played by a solo bassoon in its highest register, which transitions into a dissonant chorus of woodwinds.
