1. Fine Arts/All (New Trier Scobol Solo 2008)
Art/Archictecture It was formally known as La Cuesta Encantada, and it contains Casa del Mar, Casa del Monte, Casa del Sol, Casa Grande, an indoor Roman pool, and an outdoor Neptune pool. It used to have two runways where the current visitors center is now located. Designed by Julia Morgan to rival old European buildings, it was constantly revised by its wealthy and famous owner.
