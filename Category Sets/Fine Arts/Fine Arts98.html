1. Fine Arts/All (HAVOC II 2009)
In this 1884 painting, a man wearing a top hat sits on the grass holding a cane. A little girl in white walks with her mother while a rowing team is visible in the river. Near the man in the top hat is another man lounging with a pipe, and a dog sniffs the ground behind him. Also nearby is a girl with flowers, and several small boats can be seen in the river. In the right foreground, a woman stands holding an umbrella beside a small dog and a monkey on a leash. FTP name this pointillist work by Georges Seurat depicting a crowd of people enjoying a location on the Seine.
ANSWER: A Sunday Afternoon on the Island of La Grande Jatte [Accept Isle for Island or Un dimanche après-midi à l'Île de la Grande Jatte]
