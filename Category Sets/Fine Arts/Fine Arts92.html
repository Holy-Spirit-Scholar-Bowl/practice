1. Fine Arts/All (HSAPQ ACF 2 2008)
This man painted a figure in a blue loincloth sitting on a pink mattress and wearing a gold helm in his Mars Resting. A boy clutching a globe engages the title figure, who tends to a basin in An Old Woman Frying Eggs, and the same figure is handed a goblet filled with water from the title Seville resident in another of his works. Known for painting Innocence X, for 10 points, name this Spanish painter of Las Meninas.

12. Fine Arts/Music (PACE NSC 2013)

Fugal finales end three of his six Opus 20 "Sun" string quartets, while his Opus 76 Erdödy string quartets includes one nicknamed the "Emperor". One of his symphonies, in D major, derives its nickname from a "ticking" string and bassoon ostinato. For 10 points, name this Austrian composer of the "Clock" and "Surprise" Symphonies.
ANSWER: Franz Joseph Haydn
