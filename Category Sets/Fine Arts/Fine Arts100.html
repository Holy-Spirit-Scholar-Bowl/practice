1. Fine Arts/All (HSAPQ Tournament 17 2011)
This artist painted a woman lying on sumptuous white bed sheets below a golden cherub in his Danae, which was modeled on his wife Saskia. He painted the Hebrew letters “Mene, Mene, Tekel u-Pharsin” in his depiction of Belshazzar's Feast. He painted some inquisitive gentlemen examining a skinless arm in his painting The Anatomy Lesson of Dr. Tulp. Another of his paintings includes a girl holding a chicken and a group of men with spears and drums. For 10 points, name this Dutch artist who painted “The Shooting Company of Frans Banning Cocq” in Night Watch.
ANSWER: Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn

2. Fine Arts/All (LIST 2013)
