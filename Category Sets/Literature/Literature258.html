1. Literature/All (TJ NAREN 2010)
One character in this work has returned from a vacation in Florida, though another character notes that his mind was not rested. The central character recalls getting a job offer from his brother Ben, though he refused due to the inspiration he received from Dave Singleman. One character steals a fountain pen from Bill Oliver, though he realizes that he is unhappy in the city and longs for life on a ranch. The discovery of a tube behind a fuse box alarms Linda, and the image of stockings disturbs the main character due to his guilt over an encounter with a woman and his son Biff in Boston. For 10 points, name this play in which Willy Loman kills himself for insurance money by Arthur Miller.
ANSWER: Death of a Salesman
