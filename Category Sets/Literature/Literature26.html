4. Literature/All (PACE NSC 2006)
Without a leading article, this is the title of a poem that offers poets the chance to “pass the straits and conquer the mountains” and “double the Cape of Good Hope to some purpose.” It exhorts the soul to “steer for the deep waters only” and to “farther, farther, farther sail.” With its leading article, it is the title of a novel centering on Cyril Fielding’s reaction to the ambiguous events between Adela Quested and Dr. Aziz.
