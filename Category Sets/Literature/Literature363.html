1. Literature/World (PACE NSC 2015)

In another opera in this language, a prince pursuing a white doe is the love interest of the title character, who sings the "Song to the Moon" and is a water nymph similar to the Undine (un-DEEN). This language was used to tell the story of how Jenik (yen-ik) appears to accept money in exchange for the right to wed his lover, but instead ends up marrying Mařenka anyway.
