1. Literature/All (St. Anselms and Torrey Pines 2011)

In another play by this man, Sebastian’s death causes Catherine Holly to go insane. This author of The Rose Tattoo and Suddenly, Last Summer wrote a play in which the husband of Maggie rejects the homosexual [*] Skipper. In that play, Big Daddy is dying, and Brick Pollitt hopes to cash in. In another play by this man, Mitch dates the sister of Stanley Kowalski’s wife Stella, named Blanche DuBois. ANSWER: Tennessee Williams [or Thomas Lanier Williams]
