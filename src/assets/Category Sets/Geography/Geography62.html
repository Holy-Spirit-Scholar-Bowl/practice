1. Geography/None (SCOP Novice 2014)
This nation's port cities include Valparaiso and Antofagasta, the latter of which was invaded for its nitrate and copper resources to begin the War of the Pacific. This country is home to Easter Island and the incredibly dry Atacama Desert, which lies in a rain shadow west of the Andes Mountains.
