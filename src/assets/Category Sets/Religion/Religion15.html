1. Religion/All (HFT 2008)
The Talmud says he was “armed with a sharpened scythe” when he destroyed Sennacherib, but he was clothed in linen when he appeared with eyes “like burning lamps” to speak to a servant of the Babylonian court. 2. Religion/None (Maggie Walker GSAC 2011)
This holiday occurs on the day before the Fast of Gedaliah, and it is believed to be the day on which God inscribes one's fate into the Book of Life.
