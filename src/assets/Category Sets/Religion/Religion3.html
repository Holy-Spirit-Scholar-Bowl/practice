2. Religion/All (MW GSAC XVII 2009)
One law book of this religion is the Laws of Manu, which includes descriptions of its shraddha rites. One of the four acceptable goals of life in it is Artha, or power and wealth. One unknowable concept in this religion is called Nirguna and can only be described negatively.
