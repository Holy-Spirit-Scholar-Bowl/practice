1. Mythology/All (RAVE 2012)
The Filipino Tikbalangs and the Hindu Kinnaras are both analogs to these figures. These beings declined sour milk in favor of wine, and took the fort of Pholoe away for their own usage. While Heracles was being entertained by one of them named Pholus, he struck another one named Elatus on the arm and accidentally shot one of them in the knee. The wedding of Peleus and Thetis occurred outside one of their homes, while at the wedding of Pirithous, one of them named Eurytion had his ears cut off by Theseus after attempting to rape Hippodamia. They engaged in a notable war with the Lapiths, and one of them named Chiron trained Achilles and Jason. For 10 points, name these figures from Greek mythology that were half man and half horse.
ANSWER: centaurs (prompt on horses, on the first line)
