1. Science/Physics (PACE NSC 2015)
This person's prediction that displacement squared equals six multiplied by diffusivity multiplied by time in random walks was used by Jean Perrin to measure Avogadro's number. The proportionality constants for the rates of spontaneous and stimulated emission are named for this person. ANSWER: Albert Einstein
