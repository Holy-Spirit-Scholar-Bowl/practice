This structure also houses a mosaic at its entrance that depicts a child Christ sitting upon the lap of the Virgin Mary with a scroll in his left hand.. 18. Fine Arts/All (MOHIT (Thomas Jefferson) 2009)
This work's second movement, "Il Vecchio Castello," was written in G sharp major and 6/8 time and ostensibly represents a troubadour singing in front of a medieval castle. Other inspirations for this work include the market of Limoges, France and children in the Jardin des Tuileries. One piece in B flat major and 4/4 time, formally titled "Samuel Goldenberg und Schmuÿle," is often taken to compare a rich Jew and a poor Jew. After a piece in C minor inspired by a clock in the form of Baba-Yaga, the work proceeds to the final movement, a rondo with themes suggestive of a carillon, which is named after the never- built Great Gate of Kiev. Inspired by a tour of the artwork of Viktor Hartmann, this is, FTP, what suite of ten piano pieces composed by Modest Mussorgsky?
ANSWER: Pictures at an Exhibition
