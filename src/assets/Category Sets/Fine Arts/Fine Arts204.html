2. Fine Arts/All (PACE NSC 2001)
Ernst Barlach was one of the few sculptors to work in this style. Its precursors go back to Matthias Grünewald, but it took its modern impetus from Gauguin’s exclusion of shadows and use of folk methods, and the unique color and line stylizations of Van Gogh. For 10 points—name this art movement which abandoned Impressionism’s goal of painting that which is seen in favor of representing emotion.
ANSWER: Expressionism [or word forms]
