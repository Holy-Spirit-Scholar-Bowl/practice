1. Fine Arts/All (Crusader Cup 2005)
Toss-Up #20 A successful stock broker, he moved to Copenhagen to start a business career, but returned to France without his family to paint full-time. Though friends with Cezanne and Pisarro, he was attracted by the symbolism and vitality in the art of Asia, as the influence of Japanese prints in his “Yellow Christ” suggests.
