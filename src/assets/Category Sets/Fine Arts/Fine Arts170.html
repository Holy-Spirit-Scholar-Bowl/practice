This man's second symphony features two tam-tams of contrasting timbres being played against one another in its final movement. 6. Fine Arts/All (New Trier Scobol Solo 2009)
Music (Note to moderator: Phrygian is pronounced FRIJ-ee-un.) The four church ones, often used in Gregorian chants, were named protus, deuterus, tritus, and tetrardus.
