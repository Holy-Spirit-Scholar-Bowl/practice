1. Fine Arts/All (HSAPQ 4Q 1 2008)
This student of Jacques Ochard and Eugene Boudin showed his wife Camille Doncieux in his The Woman in the Green Dress. The artist of a series depicting a structure that houses the heart of Richard I of England, this man also created a series that includes thaw and snow effects. Painted while living at Argenteuil, one of his works depicts the port of Le Havre, and many of his other works were painted in Giverny. The creator of the Rouen Cathedral and Haystacks series, for 10 points, name this French artist of Water Lilies and Impression: Sunrise.
ANSWER: Claude Monet

FTP, name this composer who was married to Clara.

3. Fine Arts/All (Bulldog High School Academic Tournament (BHSAT) 2008)
This instrument is the alto member of its family, but its tenor and bass siblings are rarely in use today. It is pitched in F and its modern range spans from E or E flat to the C that is 2 octaves above middle C. Rarely performed as a solo instrument, it is however common given extensive lyrical solo passage in orchestral works, most famously in the Largo movement of Dvorak's 9th Symphony, the New World Symphony.
