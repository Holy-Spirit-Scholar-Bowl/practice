1. Literature/American (PACE NSC 2014)

In a novel by this author, Fanny Beaufort's fiancé Dallas visits Paris to meet with his father's former lover. In another novel by this author of "Roman Fever", a debt to Gus Trenor and the schemes of Bertha Dorset ruin the social standing of the object of Lawrence Selden's love, Lily Bart.
