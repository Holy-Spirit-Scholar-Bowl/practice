1. Literature/All (PACE NSC 2003)
After graduating from the College de Genève in 1915, this author spent time in Spain, where he became closely affiliated with the Ultraist movement. Returning to his home country, he wrote perplexing lines such as “Anyone who has read a word of Don Quixote is Miguel Cervantes” in works including The Library of Babel. For 10 points—name this blind Argentinean author of “El Aleph” and Ficciones.
ANSWER: Jorge Luis Borges
