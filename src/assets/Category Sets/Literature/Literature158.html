1. Literature/All (BHSAT 2012)
In one scene in this work, an old slave is summoned from the countryside to testify about a murder, while in another, a king accuses a blind soothsayer of being in league with his brother-in-law. A messenger from Corinth brings the news that Polybus has died in this play, while the title character recalls killing an older man at a crossroads. After the suicide of Jocasta, Creon brings in the two daughters of the newly-blind title character. For 10 points, name this play, in which the title king of Thebes realizes that he has killed his father and married his mother, a work by Sophocles.
ANSWER: Oedipus Rex [or Oedipus the King, or Oedipus Tyrannos, or Oedipus the Tyrant]

2. Literature/All (PACE NSC 2003)
Many critics did not like the ending, in which the virtuous attendant Paulina reveals all along that she had been pretending Queen Hermione was dead. Paulina arrived at the court of Sicily with Camillo, who had fled to Bohemia long before. All except one were given a happy end with the wedding of Perdita and Florizel: even the jealous King Leontes and his old friend Polixenes. For 10 points—in what play does the sole unfortunate resolution come for Antigonus, who is given the stage direction “Exit, pursued by a bear”?
ANSWER: The Winter’s Tale [begrudgingly accept A Winter’s Tale]
