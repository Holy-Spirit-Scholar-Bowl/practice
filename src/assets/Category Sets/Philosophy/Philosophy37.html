1. Philosophy/None (SCOP Novice 2012)
This author defines injustice as the breaking of a covenant, leaving everything else as just. In the same work, he describes a world without arts, letters, or industry; that world is avoided if people enter into a social contract under a sovereign with absolute power, so that life would not be "solitary, poor, nasty, brutish, and short" as it is in the state of nature. For 10 points, name this English philosopher, the author of Leviathan.
ANSWER: Thomas Hobbes
