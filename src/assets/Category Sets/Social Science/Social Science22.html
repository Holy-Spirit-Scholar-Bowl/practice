1. Social Science/None (Maggie Walker GSAC 2011)
This anthropologist studied the cranial size differences between immigrants and their children. He studied differing pronunciation in his essay "On Alternating Sounds," and he conducted research on the migration patterns of the Inuit on Baffin Island.
